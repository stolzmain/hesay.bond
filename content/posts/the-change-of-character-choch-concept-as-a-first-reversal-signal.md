## What is Change of Character

The term literally translates as a change in the character of price movement. Unlike classic reversal patterns, CHoCH focuses on market microstructure. In an uptrend, CHoCH is identified the moment price breaks and closes below the last local higher low that led to the formation of the latest high. In a downtrend, the process is mirrored: the pattern is activated when the last local lower high is breached, which followed the extreme low. Such an event indicates that buyers or sellers are no longer capable of holding key levels, and the balance of supply and demand begins to shift in favor of the opposing side.

## The Difference Between CHoCH and BOS

For analysis, it is important not to confuse a change of character with a break of structure (BOS). A BOS represents the update of a local extreme in the direction of the existing trend, confirming its strength and indicating continued movement. CHoCH, however, is always directed against the current trend. It is a primary, leading signal of a potential reversal. If a BOS indicates trend continuation, a CHoCH warns that the rules of the game are changing and the previous trend is likely finished or entering a deep correction.

## Mechanics of a Safe Entry

A true CHoCH rarely occurs spontaneously. It is usually preceded by a liquidity sweep behind a key level or a test of a Point of Interest (POI) on a higher timeframe. A trader does not necessarily need to open a trade the moment the break occurs. A conservative entry can be executed on a pullback to the formed order block or Fair Value Gap (FVG) that served as the impulse for the change in character. This allows for minimizing the stop-loss by placing it behind the new extreme, thus optimizing the risk-to-reward ratio.

## Filtering False Signals

To reduce the number of false triggers, it is important to always consider the market context. A CHoCH on lower timeframes should be aligned with the price direction on higher intervals; otherwise, it will not yield the desired results. A single level breakout without volume confirmation or prior liquidity collection often turns out to be nothing more than a false breakout. Utilizing the Change of Character concept requires the trader to exercise strict discipline, patience, and an understanding of the big picture. With a systematic and conscious approach, this analytical tool becomes a vital element for identifying reversal points in a timely manner and can significantly increase the overall effectiveness of your trading strategy.
